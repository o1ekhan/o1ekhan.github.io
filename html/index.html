Welcome To Back in Time!

Website about our life without technologies.

What if digital technologies never existed? It is hard to imagine a world without digital technology. We are so reliant on computers, the internet, automobiles, and other digital devices that it is difficult even to imagine life without them. But what if digital technologies never existed? What would the world be like? This opinion will explore what life would be like without digital technologies. Spoiler alert: it would be pretty different! Stay tuned to learn more.
